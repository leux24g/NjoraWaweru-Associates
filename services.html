Practice Areas

We believe in guiding our clients and walking the journey with them to achieve their personal and business goals.

  • Acquisition and disposition
  • Mortgage finance

  • Fund formation and investment
  • Permitting

Banking & Finance

With a wide range of expertise in general transactional banking, leveraged syndicated and acquisition finance to
restructurings and real estate finance. We represent lenders, borrowers, private equity funds, real estate and
umbrella funds as well as institutional investors, in a wide range of international transactions.

Family & Matrimonial

Protecting yourself, your family and navigating your way through the complex issues that require probate and administration.
Whether you are planning your future, require assistance during a difficult time or need someone to fight your corner, we have the legal expertise to support and guide you through whatever this may involve.

Procurement Law

Njora Waweru & Associates has a strong background in public sector work and this coupled with our leading corporate and commercial practices makes the firm ideally placed to advise when the public sector engages in commercial activity.

Conveyancing

We personally handle the legal transfer of property from one owner to another. The conveyancing process starts when the buyers' offer on a property gets accepted by the seller. We undertake the legal process until the buyer gets the property keys and vice versa

Tax Law
